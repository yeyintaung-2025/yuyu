ချေးငွေ ဝန်ဆောင်မှုအမျိုးအစားများ

အုပ်စုချေးငွေ (Group Loan)

Group-based loans for individuals starting or growing small businesses with peer accountability and support.

တစ်ဦးချင်းချေးငွေ (Individual Loan)

Tailored loan products for individual borrowers expanding their business or working independently.

ကိုရီးယားအလုပ်သမားချေးငွေ (Korea Worker Loan)

For individuals approved by the Ministry of Labour to work in Korea. Loan amounts range from 1,000,000 to 8,000,000 MMK depending on their needs and situation.

စိုက်ပျိုးမွေးမြူရေးချေးငွေ(Agri Loan)
